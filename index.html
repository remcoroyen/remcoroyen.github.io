Publications

Research interests are in 3D computer vision, deep learning, and image processing. Most of my research has been focused on 3D scene understanding and the introduction of scalability to deep learning. Currently, I have also developed an interest in the impressive development of view synthesis methods and its connection with existing 3D processing methods. Representative papers for my research are highlighted.

Joint prototype and coefficient prediction for 3d instance segmentation (in press)
Remco Royen, Leon Denis, Adrian Munteanu
IET Electronics Letters, 2024
paper

A 3D instance segmentation method which simultaneously learns coefficients and prototypes. The obtained prototypes are visualizable and interpretable.

W6dnet: Weakly-supervised domain adaptation for monocular vehicle 6d pose estimation with 3d priors and synthetic data (in press)
Yangxintong Lyu, Remco Royen, Adrian Munteanu
IEEE Transactions on Instrumentation and Measurement, 2024
paper

A new synthetic dataset, SynthV6D, enabling 6D pose estimation of vehicles in monocular traffic images, is presented. Secondly, a weakly-supervised domain adaptation approach to recover the 6D pose is introduced. Experiments show that with a small amount of real-world data available, the proposed approach can significantly advance the performance when adapting knowledge from SynthV6D.

Pcgen: a fully parallelizable point cloud generative model (in press)

MDPI Sensors, 2024
paper

A fully parallelizable vector-quantized variational autoencoder model (VQVAE) that generates high-quality point clouds in milliseconds.

RESSCAL3D: Resolution Scalable 3D Semantic Segmentation of Point Clouds
Remco Royen, Adrian Munteanu
IEEE International Conference on Image Processing (ICIP), 2023
paper

RESSCAL3D provides resolution-scalable 3D semantic segmentation of point clouds. By enabling the processing of different resolution scales in parallel, RESSCAL3D is 31-62% faster than the non-scalable baseline and allows early predictions.

Improved Block Merging for 3D Point Cloud Instance Segmentation
Leon Denis, Remco Royen, Adrian Munteanu
International Conference on Digital Signal Processing (DSP), 2023
paper

A novel block merging algorithm suitable for any block-based 3D instance segmentation technique is proposed. The experiments show that it significantly and consistently improves the obtained accuracy for all evaluation metrics employed in literature, regardless of the underlying network architecture.

MONO6D: Monocular Vehicle 6D Pose Estimation with 3D Priors
Yangxintong Lyu, Remco Royen, Adrian Munteanu
IEEE International Conference on Image Processing (ICIP), 2022
paper

A novel monocular approach for vehicle pose estimation using vehicle 3D priors provided by vehicle make-and-model recognition methods to estimate the 6D pose. The experimental results show that the proposed method is superior to the state-of-the-art approaches in both objective and subjective terms.

MaskLayer: Enabling scalable deep learning solutions by training embedded feature sets
Remco Royen, Leon Denis, Quentin Bolsee, Pengpeng Hu, Adrian Munteanu
Neural Networks, 2021
paper

MaskLayer is a new neural network layer. It can be integrated in any feedforward network, allowing quality scalability by design by creating embedded feature sets. To further improve the performance, a masked optimizer and a balancing gradient rescaling approach are proposed. Our experiments show that the cost of introducing scalability using MaskLayer remains limited while important benefits are presented.
